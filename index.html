Do You Know!

The Eiffel Tower in Paris is an icon of love and architecture. Standing 330 meters tall, it offers breathtaking views of the city. Built in 1889 for the World’s Fair, it has since become one of the most visited monuments in the world.

Stretching over 21,000 km, the Great Wall of China is one of the greatest architectural wonders in history. It was built to protect ancient China from invasions and today stands as a symbol of strength and endurance.

Santorini is world-famous for its whitewashed houses, blue-domed churches, and stunning sunsets over the Aegean Sea. Its volcanic history and picturesque villages make it one of the most romantic travel destinations.
